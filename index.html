Our Products

Grey Back Duplex Board

High-Grade Greyback Duplex Paper Boards

Greyback Duplex Paper Boards, as the name suggests, are made with different grades of pulp layers. The top layer uses high-graded recycled pulp, while the bottom layer is made of grey paper waste. Available in various sheet sizes and thicknesses from 230 GSM to 500 GSM. We also supply customer-centric requirements in terms of reels and sheets.

White Back Duplex Board

Premium Whiteback Duplex Paper Boards

White Back Duplex is a premium duplex paper board made of high-graded recycled pulp and top-quality surface treatment. Featuring white virgin waste layers, these coated boards are perfect for packaging high-quality products with a shiny, dust-proof surface. Available in thicknesses from 230 GSM to 450 GSM.
